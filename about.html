First steps of the Investor

Investor News Agency started off in 1960 as a small local newspaper brand that mainly published coverage on the financial affairs in the region of Hampshire, UK. At around that time period, the economic indicators had just started to depict improvements since the end of WWII. Hence, it was a crucial time to reflect on these new financial horizons and to address any economic fluctuations. From the opening of a new store in town to the latest fiscal policies implemented by the government, the Investor grew in size and could now obtain information regarding all.

As time passed, the Investor News Agency developed its operations. Now with more than 6 offices in the UK and another 10 in Europe, the Investor is a leading financial news publisher. Offering millions of newspapers and magazines per year and now providing various subscription plans for the new e-news website, we are sure to remain in the top financial news publishers for the foreseeable future.

Awards

PPA Independent Publisher Awards

This award is given once a year to each of the top five independent financial publishers in the UK. It demonstrates the ability of the news agency to conduct private investigations into sensitive matters and to correctly analyse economic circumstance on the basis of political affairs in the field of international relations.

Digital Media Awards

The Digital Media Awards event is an event ran every four years which surveys the UKâ€™s news publishers and their digital presence. Only the most updated in terms of technological coverage and accessibility are awarded with this honour. The Investor has been awarded for the new website design, the creation of a mobile application and the ability of the application to read out loud the articles to the user. This is indeed a needed feature in some cases.

Readly Award

The Annual Readly Awards take place in Brussels and compares the most popular news and media companies in Europe. The main criteria for this award is for the company to have an explicitly expressed tone of voice and a method of writing their pieces. It is also a scrutinized criterion to have zero to none indices of plagiarism of any other news publisher. The investor is proud to announce it has been granted this award as one of the few in Brussels in 2020.

Top Publisher Award 2020

The Top Publisher Award 2020 is a brand-new international competition in which all news publishers from around the world can participate. There is a participation fee however, which is at the end donated entirely in its full amount to an organisation that helps poor children get a proper education. This award is for being a publisher company which can show evidence of hiring without discrimination and treats its employees fairly, which provides many opportunities for interns to acquire experience in the area and which works closely with universities and other institutions to proliferate education and journalism expertise.
